Version Compatibility

  • iOS 8.0-8.4. All iPhones and iPod touches. iPad compatibility is planned.

Description

  • I swore off making icon themes, but I couldn't stay away for long. This is Mugi, a vibrant theme based on gradients and Adobe's iOS app icons.

    This theme is currently in beta and still very much a work in progress. I'll be posting regular updates and constantly changing things, so I appreciate your patience and welcome you aboard for the ride. Once I'm happy with the quality and number of icons, I'll probably put this on a default repo for free.

    When I'm not drinking tea and eating cake, I'll push updates once every one or two weeks. Come fall when classes start again, it'll be reduced to approximately once a month. You can expect updates at the end of the week, usually on Fridays or Saturdays.

    Your feedback is greatly appreciated, so if you feel like a certain icon needs changing, please let me know, and I'll take it into consideration! If you would like a certain icon with a different background color, I can make that for you as well.

    I am not taking icon requests at this time. Let me get a decent number of icons done first. When I'm ready to take icon requests, I'll open up a form and make it available.

    I took a lot of inspiration from Axla and this unfinished concept by Meteochu and Muze by Purdix. Thank you as well to Djaovx (Enkel) for the feedback during the design process. Go buy their themes and follow them on Twitter, they're super awesome people!
