Version Compatability

  • iOS 8.x

Description

  • Zuiver is a simplistic and elegant flat widget which includes the time, date, temperature, location and weather conditions.

    The theme includes a number of customisable elements. For details on customising this theme please refer to the Reddit post which is linked to below.

    Zuiker requires GroovyLock or LockHTML 3/4 for the lockscreen and SBHTML for the springboard.

    An extremely special thank you goes to @hizinfiz for allowing me to put this on his repo (plus the guidance to help me do so) and for the wonderful moderation of /r/jailbreak and /r/iosthemes.
